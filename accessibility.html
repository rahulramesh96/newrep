Accessibility

NASCARE is committed to making its Website, https://nascare.netlify.app/, more accessible and usable for all individuals, especially those with impairments. To that end, NASCARE has committed to operating the Online in significant accordance with commonly known and acceptable rules and/or standards for website accessibility. The World Wide Web Consortium's Web Content Accessibility Guidelines 2.0 Level AA (WCAG 2.0) are now the most widely accepted website accessibility standard.

In accordance with technological advancements, NASCARE will continue to take the steps necessary to maintain substantial conformance with WCAG 2.0 (or any successor generally recognized website accessibility standard), such as conducting website accessibility audit(s) and providing related training regarding website design and maintenance.
