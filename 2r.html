Reuse & Recycle

NASCARE recycles plastics, paper, cans, and has old phone recycling kiosks at the tracks. Sprint (the phone provider) receives the old phones from NASCAR and recycles them.

Coca-Cola and Miller Coors are both NASCAR partners who help recycle and educate fans about the benefits of recycling. Coca-Cola has installed over 10,000 recycling containers at locations where the product is marketed. Instead of being placed near the merchants, these containers have been placed throughout the track. There are now recycling bins in the stands, suites, garages, and campers.

NASCARE recycles used tires that are no longer usable. The used tires are turned into spongy playground surfaces that protect youngsters from injury when they fall. Mulch and mud have been replaced by this new flooring. This solution is not only more environmentally friendly, but also safer. If a child falls on mulch, they may receive a splinter or, worse, a piece of bark in their hand.

Used tires can also be combined with other elements to create asphalt for roadways.
